About 3D Reading Map

A revolutionary way to visualize and understand the interconnected world of literature.

Our Vision

The 3D Reading Map transforms how readers discover and connect with books by representing literature as a dynamic, spatial network.

Launched in 2020, we've mapped over 10,000 classic and contemporary works, revealing hidden connections across genres, cultures, and time periods.

Using advanced visualization techniques, our platform helps students, educators, and book lovers see literature in a whole new dimension.
